About Us

The Snowflake Ski Club is much more than just skiing. We're a year-round, non-profit, volunteer-run social and outdoor activities organization with a variety of events designed to appeal to just about everybody. The Club has grown a lot since its beginning in 1948, when it consisted of a few hardy souls carpooling the 8 to 10 hour drive to the rope tow at Flagstaff. In fact, the history of the Club is really a history of skiing in Arizona.

Now, with over 250 members (which keeps growing), the Club runs ski trips each winter all over the Western US In addition to skiing, you'll find us hiking, white water rafting, bicycling, houseboating, canoeing, 4-wheel driving, touring the wine country, taking in the theatre, whooping it up at musical events, water skiing, and just socializing.

Check out our activities and our ski trips. Visit us at one of our regular meetings. You'll find we're a friendly group, constantly on the go. Subscribe to our email mailing list for up-to-date information on our activities.
